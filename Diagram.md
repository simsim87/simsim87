    SetGoals --> DevelopPlan[Develop a Strategic Plan]
    DevelopPlan --> TakeAction[Take Consistent Action]
    TakeAction -->|Success| AchieveGoals[Achieve Goals]
    TakeAction -->|Struggles| Evaluate[Evaluate Progress]
    Evaluate --> AdjustPlan[Adjust Plan as Necessary]
    AdjustPlan --> TakeAction
    AchieveGoals --> Celebrate[Celebrate Success]
    Celebrate --> End([End])
## Entity Descriptions

1. **Start**: Initiates the process of achieving work success.
2. **Set Clear Goals**: Establishes specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) objectives.
3. **Develop a Strategic Plan**: Outlines a structured approach to reach the set goals.
4. **Take Consistent Action**: Involves executing the strategic plan through regular efforts.
5. **Evaluate Progress**: Assesses the effectiveness of the actions taken.
6. **Adjust Plan as Necessary**: Revises strategies based on evaluation outcomes.
7. **Achieve Goals**: Marks the successful completion of defined objectives.
8. **Celebrate Success**: Acknowledges achievements and boosts morale.
9. **End**: Concludes the entire process.

## Relationship Significance

- **Start ➔ Set Clear Goals**: Establishes the foundation for the entire process, ensuring that all actions are directed towards clear objectives.
- **Set Clear Goals ➔ Develop a Strategic Plan**: Ensures that the strategic plan aligns with the goals, providing a roadmap for success.
- **Develop a Strategic Plan ➔ Take Consistent Action**: Translates planning into actionable steps, facilitating progress toward the goals.
